Our Story

Özçelik Coffee was founded in 1985, with a passion for bringing the finest coffee to our customers. From the highest quality beans to sustainable sourcing, our journey is all about sharing our love for coffee with you.

Our Mission

We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere, where every cup is crafted with care and every customer leaves with a smile. Our mission is to provide not only great coffee but also a great experience.
